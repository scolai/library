# 🟩 **Bing Webmaster Tools flags short titles because…**
- Longer titles tend to include more context  
- They improve click‑through rate  
- They help disambiguate pages  
- They give Bing more semantic signals  

But **Bing does NOT require 60 characters**.  
It’s a *guideline*, not a ranking factor.

That said, for your site — bilingual, service‑based, trust‑driven — longer titles are actually a strategic advantage.

Let’s turn this into an opportunity.

# 🟦 What Bing *really* wants in a title
A strong title should include:

- **Primary keyword**  
- **Secondary keyword or context**  
- **Brand name**  
- **A value signal** (optional)

And for your Japanese audience:

- **Clear service type**  
- **Location**  
- **安心 / 日本語対応** signals  

When you combine these, you naturally hit 60+ characters.
